About us

Student Led

Like the other RAHS school teams, team members elect student leaders and we write the team constitution.

Students Design and Build Robots

We are grateful for our school staff and community mentors who generously volunteer their time to guide us through building our competition robot.

Mission Statement

Our goal is to focus on engineering principals, leadership, and teamwork with passionate and dedicated team members through the guidance of mentors. Students design and build a robot to compete in FIRST competitions. Spread FIRST in our community and have a fun experience.

Inspire a love of STEM and FIRST

Through community and district outreach, we teach and inspire the next generation to open their mind to the world of STEM and FIRST.
